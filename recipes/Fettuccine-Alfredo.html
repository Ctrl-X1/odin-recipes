Fettuccine Alfredo

Recipe Description

Fettuccine Alfredo is a rich, creamy Italian-American pasta dish made by tossing hot, cooked fettuccine noodles in a sauce of melted butter, grated Parmesan cheese, and starchy pasta water.

While authentic Roman versions rely solely on the emulsion of butter, cheese, and pasta water for creaminess, American variations frequently add heavy cream, garlic, and sometimes nutmeg for a thicker sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta.
  2. Melt the butter and cream together on the stove, season, and stir in the cheese.
  3. Toss the pasta in the cheese sauce.
